This one is my favourite.

I will bite my lip and pour blood down your throat,
On cold nights like this, I will be your fuzzy coat.
You make my heart beat faster, my pulse on the rise.
Oh, I have looked at you, Bhavya.
No amount of beauty can now satisfy my eyes.
I will condense the moisture in your breath,
Turn it into droplets on your skin.
You bring me the sweet comfort of assurance,
The thrill of committing a sin.
Oh, I am drunk in your love.
The sweet taste of sanctity and crime.
The taste of religion and of devotion.
The taste of lust and of grime.
