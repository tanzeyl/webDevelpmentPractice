Okay, so this poem is a little dark.

I slit open my wrists,
And all that pours out is your name.
I put it to my lips,
And have a mouthful of your taste.

I bleed a river and I don't stop.
I drown myself on the shore.
My love is dark, my love is haunting.
My love is violence, my love is gore.

You are the darkness surrounding my heart.
You are the scars on my soul.
The part and parcel of my aura,
You graze me and make me whole.
